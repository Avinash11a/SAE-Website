About Us

SAE India collegiate chapter of NIT Durgapur was conceived in 2007 with an idea to integrate the students of the college into international fabric of automobile engineering. Presently 200 students are enjoying the benefits of SAE India membership.
We organize a number of innovative events, workshops and lectures round the year and a competitive place where the sharpest minds battle in the quest to achieve disctinction. The amalgamation of all these events under a single roof, during the month of February finally results in the onique odyssey called 'Motor Zundung' which is the largest Auto fest of Eastern India. SAE NIT Durgapur also publishes its annual magazine called 'Autopia'.

About BAJA

BAJA SAE India is a rapidly growing intercollegiate automoble design and fabrication competetion organized by SAE that spans the whole country with participation of over 400 reputed engineering colleges. Each team's goal is to design and build a prototype of a rugged single seater, four wheeled ATV. It has 2 stages: Virtual BAJA & Main event.
